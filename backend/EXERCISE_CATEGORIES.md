# PowerFit Exercise Categories

## Categories Overview

The exercise library is organized by **6 main muscle groups**:

## 1. Chest Exercises

### Primary Focus: Pectoralis Major (Chest Muscles)

**Beginner:**
- Push-ups (Bodyweight)

**Intermediate:**
- Dumbbell Flyes (Dumbbells)
- Incline Bench Press (Barbell)

**Advanced:**
- Bench Press (Barbell)
- Close-Grip Bench Press

### Secondary Muscles Trained
- Triceps
- Anterior Deltoids (Front Shoulders)
- Serratus Anterior (Side Ribs)

### Equipment Needed
- Barbell and bench
- Dumbbells
- Machines
- Cables
- Bodyweight

### Best Rep Ranges
- Strength: 3-6 reps
- Hypertrophy (Size): 8-12 reps
- Endurance: 12-15+ reps

---

## 2. Back Exercises

### Primary Focus: Latissimus Dorsi (Lats)

**Beginner:**
- Lat Pulldowns (Machine)
- Inverted Rows (Bodyweight)

**Intermediate:**
- Barbell Rows
- Dumbbell Rows
- Cable Rows

**Advanced:**
- Pull-ups (Bodyweight)
- Deadlifts (Barbell)
- Weighted Pull-ups

### Secondary Muscles Trained
- Biceps (Arm Flexors)
- Rhomboids (Mid-Back)
- Trapezius (Upper Back)
- Rear Deltoids (Back Shoulders)
- Erector Spinae (Lower Back)

### Equipment Needed
- Pull-up bar
- Barbell
- Dumbbells
- Cables
- Machines

### Best Rep Ranges
- Strength: 3-6 reps
- Hypertrophy: 8-12 reps
- Endurance: 12-15+ reps

---

## 3. Leg Exercises

### Primary Focus: Quadriceps & Glutes

**Beginner:**
- Bodyweight Squats
- Lunges (Bodyweight)
- Leg Press (Machine)

**Intermediate:**
- Barbell Squats
- Bulgarian Split Squats
- Romanian Deadlifts
- Leg Extensions

**Advanced:**
- Heavy Barbell Squats
- Deadlifts (Full Body)
- Pistol Squats

### Secondary Muscles Trained
- Hamstrings (Back of Thigh)
- Adductors (Inner Thigh)
- Calves
- Core/Abs
- Erector Spinae (Lower Back)

### Equipment Needed
- Squat rack
- Barbell
- Dumbbells
- Leg press machine
- Leg extension/curl machines
- Bodyweight

### Best Rep Ranges
- Strength: 3-6 reps
- Hypertrophy: 8-12 reps
- Endurance: 15-20+ reps (higher reps safer for knees)

### Injury Modifications
- **Knee Pain:** Box squats, leg press, short range
- **Lower Back:** Goblet squats, leg press, avoid deadlifts
- **Ankle Issues:** Heels elevated squats, leg press

---

## 4. Shoulder Exercises

### Primary Focus: Deltoids (Shoulder Muscles)

**Anterior (Front) Deltoids:**
- Overhead Press (Barbell)
- Front Raises (Dumbbells)

**Medial (Middle) Deltoids:**
- Lateral Raises (Dumbbells)
- Machine Lateral Raises

**Posterior (Rear) Deltoids:**
- Face Pulls (Cables)
- Reverse Flyes (Dumbbells)
- Band Pull-aparts (Resistance Band)

### Beginner Exercises
- Lateral Raises (Dumbbells)
- Face Pulls (Cables)
- Band Pull-aparts (Band)

### Intermediate Exercises
- Overhead Press (Barbell)
- Dumbbell Shoulder Press
- Arnold Press

### Advanced Exercises
- Heavy Overhead Press
- Pike Push-ups
- Handstand Work

### Secondary Muscles Trained
- Triceps
- Upper Chest
- Trapezius
- Rotator Cuff (Small stabilizers)

### Equipment Needed
- Dumbbells
- Barbell
- Cables
- Machines
- Resistance Bands
- Bodyweight

### Best Rep Ranges
- Strength: 6-8 reps
- Hypertrophy: 8-12 reps
- Endurance: 12-15+ reps
- **High reps preferred** (shoulders do well with 10-15 reps)

### Injury Modifications
- **Shoulder Impingement:** Neutral grip, machines, lighter weight
- **Rotator Cuff Issues:** Band work, face pulls, avoid overhead
- **Dislocated Shoulder:** Physio exercises only until cleared

---

## 5. Arm Exercises

### Biceps (Front of Arm)

**Beginner:**
- Barbell Curls
- Dumbbell Curls

**Intermediate:**
- Hammer Curls
- Cable Curls
- Preacher Curls

**Advanced:**
- Close-Grip Curls
- Single-Arm Dumbbell Curls
- 21s (Advanced technique)

### Triceps (Back of Arm)

**Beginner:**
- Tricep Pushdowns (Cable)
- Close-Grip Push-ups

**Intermediate:**
- Tricep Dips
- Skull Crushers
- Overhead Extensions

**Advanced:**
- Weighted Dips
- Close-Grip Bench Press
- Heavy Skull Crushers

### Equipment Needed
- Dumbbells
- Barbell
- EZ-Curls Bar
- Cables
- Dip Station
- Bench

### Best Rep Ranges
- Strength: 6-8 reps
- Hypertrophy: 8-12 reps (especially for arms)
- Endurance: 12-20+ reps
- **Arms respond best to 8-12 and 12-15 rep ranges**

### Injury Modifications
- **Tennis Elbow:** Light cables, hammer curls
- **Golfer's Elbow:** Avoid heavy curling
- **Wrist Pain:** EZ-Curl bar, machine curls

---

## 6. Core Exercises

### Rectus Abdominis (Six-Pack Abs)
- Crunches (Bodyweight)
- Cable Crunches
- Ab Wheel Rollouts

### Obliques (Side Abs)
- Russian Twists (Weight)
- Side Planks
- Cable Wood Chops
- Suitcase Carries

### Transverse Abdominis (Deep Core)
- Planks (Isometric)
- Dead Bugs
- Bird Dogs
- Pallof Press

### Lower Back (Posterior Chain)
- Deadlifts (Heavy compound)
- Good Mornings
- Back Extensions
- Superman Holds

### Beginner Core
- Plank (Isometric)
- Dead Bug
- Bird Dog

### Intermediate Core
- Russian Twists
- Side Plank
- Ab Wheel (Knee)

### Advanced Core
- Hanging Leg Raises
- Ab Wheel (Full)
- Dragon Flags
- Toes-to-Bar

### Best Rep Ranges
- **Isometric (Plank):** 30-60 seconds
- **Dynamic:** 12-20+ reps
- **Hanging:** 8-15 reps

### Equipment Needed
- Bodyweight
- Ab Wheel
- Dumbbells
- Medicine Ball
- Pull-up Bar
- Machines
- Cables

### Injury Modifications
- **Lower Back Pain:** Avoid heavy deadlifts, use machines
- **Neck Issues:** Avoid crunches, use planks
- **Knee Issues:** Avoid leg raises, use dead bugs

---

## Training Split Examples

### Full Body (3x/week)
- Chest + Back
- Legs + Core
- Shoulders + Arms

### Upper/Lower (4x/week)
- Upper A: Chest, Back, Shoulders
- Lower A: Quadriceps, Hamstrings, Glutes
- Upper B: Back, Shoulders, Arms
- Lower B: Hamstrings, Glutes, Calves

### Push/Pull/Legs (6x/week)
- Push: Chest, Shoulders, Triceps
- Pull: Back, Biceps
- Legs: Quadriceps, Hamstrings, Glutes, Calves
